Security

We are aware that some of our customers have received email messages about fake invoices which may contain a harmful file attachment.

Tesco Bank takes security very seriously and will never ask you to disclose your security information through email. Our Security Centre contains guidance to help you protect your computer from viruses and malware as well as providing information on how you can protect yourself.

Remember to keep your banking details safe!

  • Never tell anyone your security details.
  • Never respond to an email asking for your personal security information.
  • If you think you have received a fraudulent email, please forward it to phishing@tescobank.com and then delete it.

...and keep your computer safe too

  • Install and maintain up-to-date anti-virus and spyware protection.
  • Install TrustDefender, our FREE protection software.
  • Install and activate a personal firewall.
  • Keep your browser software up to date.

Find out more by visiting our Security Centre.
