    WELCOME TO BTEC PVT. LTD.

    BTEC Pvt. Ltd. established in 1979 is a first name to supply various types of quality Components to India’s Largest OEM’s like TATA Motors, Mahindra & Mahindra, John Deere, Siemens, Marathon Motors, Oerlikon Fairfield, and Weber Hydraulic India Pvt. Ltd. etc.

    Experience that spans around 35 years of manufacturing, a rigid adherence to high quality standards, a willingness to adopt sophisticated state of art, technology and a strong infrastructure have been major factors, which have successfully contributed to company’s growth.

    BTEC has an impressive track of growth by investing in the latest manufacturing technologies, Increasing it’s inhouse capabilities and providing training for it’s employees to achieve reliable products at competitive price with better products flexibilities consistancy and optimum service.

    BTEC fittings are manufactured by using advance machining and engineering technology, on ultra modern automates, multi-spindle SPM’s & CNC machines by highly skilled team of engineers and operators made at par with various international norms confirming to the worldwide testing standards.

    They key strength by which BTEC stands apart is a focused improvement in quality and accuracy along with productivity of parts. constant improvement is way of life at BTEC we always look to improve our products and provide our customers the best.
