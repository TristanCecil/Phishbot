About M&T Bank
M&T Bank has been committed to customers and community for more than 150 years. Founded on the principle of providing exceptional financial products and friendly, personalized service, M&T is more than just your neighborhood bank. Let us help you reach your financial goals with our full suite of bank accounts and lending solutions, including savings accounts, checking accounts, auto and home loans, and more.

At M&T, we succeed when our customers succeed, because we understand what’s important. To learn more about the banking products and solutions that are right for you, or to open a savings or checking account, find your local M&T branch or call our knowledgeable customer service representatives at 1-800-724-2440.

So go ahead—raise the green flag with M&T Bank to start making your money work harder for you.
