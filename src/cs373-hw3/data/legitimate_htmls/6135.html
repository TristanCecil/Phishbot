US New Home Sales At 8-Year High

New single-family home sales in US jumped 16.6% to 619,000 in April, much higher than an upwardly revised 531,000 in March and compared to market expectations of 523,000. It is the highest reading since January of 2008 and the biggest gain since 1992.
