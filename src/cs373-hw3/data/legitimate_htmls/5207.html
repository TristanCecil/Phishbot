What's OSM?

Online Soccer Manager is the game in which you can become the manager of your favorite soccer club. Show the world that you are the best soccer manager! Join a league or start one of your own along with your friends.

Choose your favorite club
You want to compete for the championship with a top club or like to play against relegation? Everything is possible. Your favorite club needs you!
Determine your Lineup
Classic wingers or two strikers? It's your call. You decide the formation, you decide who plays. Every single game!
Decide your Tactics
You're a fan of the passing game? Or you like to fall back and use the counterattack? As a tactical mastermind, you can perfect your team in all details!
Strengthen your Roster
Search the world for the best talents, negotiate with other clubs and sell your dispensable players. With a cunning transfer policy you can compose your ideal team!
Daily game
Manage your club against the competition every day. Train your players on a daily basis and play Friendlies to test your Tactics!
Play against friends
Of course, playing against friends is the best. Challenge them, join their league and compete like your life depends on it. May the best win!
