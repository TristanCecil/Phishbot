channeltraffic.net



1. What is channeltraffic.net ?

channeltraffic.net is an advertising network technology tracking domain. Advertisers distribute their ads across a broad network of search partners and content sites. Before traffic is delivered to advertisers, channeltraffic.net is used as a network referral ID to identify and qualify traffic sent by traffic partners.




2. Why am I seeing channeltraffic.net on my computer?

Because the advertising network is comprised of thousands of traffic partners, you may have been included within a partner's advertisement distribution network. If you have received any advertisements that contain the url channeltraffic.net, it is important to understand that this property and the advertisements you've seen have no association with the traffic partner that is displaying ads to you. channeltraffic.net is not the source of the advertisements.
