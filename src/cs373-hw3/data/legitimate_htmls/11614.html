    About AccountingTools

    The AccountingTools site is the complete source of information for the accountant. It contains continuing professional education (CPE) courses, accounting and finance books, the Accounting Best Practices podcast (with over 2.7 million downloads), and articles on over a thousand topics - all from accounting author Steven Bragg. Our emphasis is on clear explanations of the accounting standards and the practical management aspects of accounting.
