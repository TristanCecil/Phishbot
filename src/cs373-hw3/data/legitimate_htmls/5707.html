April jobless rates down in 5 states, up in 4; payroll jobs up in 11 states, down in 6

In April, unemployment rates were significantly lower in 5 states, higher in 4 states, and stable in 41 states and the District of Columbia. Nonfarm payroll employment increased in 11 states, decreased in 6 states, and was essentially unchanged in 33 states and the District.
