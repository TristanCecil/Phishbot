HOW IT WORKS

Get the right candidates

  • One-click posting to 15+ free and premium job boards and social networks
  • Create your own branded careers site – no coding required
  • Customize application forms for easy candidate screening

Workable helps attract more candidates, faster. You’ll save money on premium job listings too. Even better, our customizable application forms make sure you’ll get not just more candidates, but the right ones.

Streamline recruiting

  • Browse resumes in your secure online candidate database
  • Access notes, feedback and communication anywhere
  • Send bulk emails and schedule interviews

No missing emails, goodbye spreadsheets. Resumes are automatically stored in your secure candidate browser, accessible on your laptop, desktop or the smartphone in your pocket. Never miss a great candidate.

Source candidates more effectively

  • Add external recruiters in to your hiring team
  • Chrome extension for sourcing passive candidates
  • Integrates with your LinkedIn recruiter account

Sometimes the best candidates are sourced for the role. Say goodbye to data entry with the resume parser – just drag and drop resumes into the pipeline and see public and social profiles gathered automatically.
