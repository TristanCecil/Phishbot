It's May at Homes.com!

Hey there! Long time, no see. It's hard to believe it's May already, right? There's a lot going on this month. If you're a Star Wars fan, you're probably really excited about May the Fourth. We know we are. In case you haven't heard, it's the official Star Wars Day. Here at Homes.com, we'll be celebrating with a Chewbacca roar contest, dressing up like our favorite characters, and having an epic lightsaber battle. May the Fourth Be With You!

Don't forget: Mother's Day is on May 8th. We hope you'll take some time to show your mother (and any other ladies who played a big role in your life) just how much you appreciate them. Surprise her with some flowers, candy, maybe some grand kids... nothing's too good for your mom. Just kidding about the grand kids thing, unless you were going to do that anyway.

There are some great holidays toward the end of the month, too. The 24th is National Brother Day, so if you have a brother, maybe give him a noogie or buy him a gift. Memorial Day is on the 30th. Why not catch a parade, have a barbecue, or take a moment to remember our country's heroes?

However you choose to spend the month of May, we hope it's grand. And if your May plans include buying a new house, you know where to find us.
