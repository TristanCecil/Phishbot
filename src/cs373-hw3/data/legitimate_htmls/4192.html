    TALENT APPS

    • SilkRoad Recruiting

      Easy-to-use SilkRoad Recruiting (OpenHire) provides you witheverything neededto attract, screen, and hire top talent globally, increasing the quality of hire while decreasing time and cost per hire. And, even better, access the largest free job distribution in the industry.

      See Recruiting

    • SilkRoad Onboarding

      Thisground-breaking employee onboarding software solution, SilkRoad Onboarding (RedCarpet) drives engagement during every phase of the employee’s career, allowing organizations to reduce costs and time-spent on new hire training and developmentand ensuring greater employee commitment and success.

      Explore Onboarding

    • SilkRoad Performance

      The easy-to-use, paperless SilkRoad Performance Management (WingSpan) enables your organization to deliver meaningful appraisals on time. Set goals, evaluate employee skills, provide feedback and opportunities for development, and tie performance to compensation.

      Review Performance

    • SilkRoad Learning

      SilkRoad Learning (GreenLight), an award-winning Learning Management System, drives your strategic talent initiatives by delivering customized or third-party training courses in an engaging format while saving time, reducing costs, and reinforcing compliance.

      See Learning

    • SilkRoad HRMS

      The strong foundation you need to manage critical employee data in one central location. SilkRoad HRMS (HeartBeat) combines powerful security, data management and reporting, with a best in class partner ecosystem. Seamlessly manage Benefits, Payroll and PTO and more.

      Get HRMS Details

    • SilkRoad Talent Apps

      The SilkRoad Talent App Exchange Marketplace allows customers to augment their SilkRoad talent management solutions with key functions, such as background screening, benefits administration, and payroll.

      See Talent Apps
