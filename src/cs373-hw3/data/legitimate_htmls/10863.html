How does our word generator work? For our base Scrabble dictionary for the word finder we use a large word list, which is similar to the dictionary for Scrabble® Crossword game tournaments in the US and Canada. Our Word Solver works in several languages - We also use the dictionary in our Solveur Scrabble (French Scrabble® Crossword game solver), a shorter German dictionary for our Wortsuche (German Solver), A large Italian word dictionary for our Italian Scrabble® Crossword game, a gargantuan Spanish Dictionary for the Buscador Palabra, and a smaller dictionary for our Romanian Scrabble® Crossword game Helper.
