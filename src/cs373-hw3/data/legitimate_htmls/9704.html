Welcome to Halfords.com

As the UK's leading cycling and motoring retailer, Halfords is your one-stop shop for exclusive brands in bikes, high quality car parts and accessories, as well as the latest Sat Navs and travelling equipment.

Browse the latest collection of men and women's mountain bikes, road bike and hybrid bikes. If the shorter day and winter weather is stopping you from enjoying your bike rides, than check out our wide range of cycle clothing, suitable for all conditions.

We pride ourselves in offering the highest quality motoring products, so you can rest assured your vehicle will keep moving. For a small fee our professional colleagues are able to fit car batteries, wiper blades and car bulbs.

If you are planning a getaway then shop touring and camping for a wide choice of roof boxes, camping equipment and bike racks as well as TomTom and Garmin Sat Navs to ensure you safely arrive at your destination.
