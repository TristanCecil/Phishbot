CSR Activities & NGO's Initiatives

The Andhra Pradesh government is keen on unlocking the potential corporate social responsibility (CSR) funds of companies as part of concentrated efforts to build the new capital city. The initiative of Government is to coordinate and make concerted cohesive efforts in organizing CSR programmes, in association with IT industry in a coordinated and synchronized manner, so that a visible impact is seen and felt by the society AP Government is keen to have the partnership with Non-government organizations (NGOs) as well in serving citizens of the state.
