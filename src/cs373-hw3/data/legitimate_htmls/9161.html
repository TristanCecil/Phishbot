2016 Commencement Speaker

Alumnus and honorary degree recipient Harry Edwards inspired a generation to take a stand for human rights. He will address graduates at the Commencement ceremony on May 28.

2016 Outstanding Graduating Senior

Anna Santana will graduate with a double major in sociology and Spanish. This daughter of former farmworkers advocates for the education of migrant families and promotes a college-going culture.

2016 Outstanding Graduating Senior

Erin Enguero will graduate with a bachelor’s degree in kinesiology. Overcoming hearing loss, she has evolved from a self-described "cautious pre-teen to an ambitious young woman striving for excellence."

Top Ten Ranking of Best Value Colleges

A San Jose State diploma is an excellent investment providing one of the highest returns for students and their families among California schools, according to an analysis by PayScale.com.

San Jose State Receives $15 Million Gift

South Bay philanthropist Lupe Diaz Compean’s remarkable generosity will support student success initiatives, scholarships and student programming in the newly renovated Student Union.
