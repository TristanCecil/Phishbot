HUSK is a Silent Hill-inspired horror game from the developers of Kholat

NewsApril 22, 2016 - by luckie

Polish studio IMGN.pro, the team that gave us Kholat, presents HUSK, an FPP narrative horror inspired by Silent Hill and Alan Wake. Developed by UndeadScout, HUSK will be set in a small American town called Shivercliff, which hides a second, very terrifying nature. While exploring the place you will have to fight enemies, solve puzzles, and discover the dark mystery of the town. HUSK runs on Unreal Engine 4, and will feature music by Arkadiusz Reikowski, composer of the soundtracks for Layers of Fear and Kholat.

Ruiner – cyberpunk top-down shooter on awesome first trailer

NewsApril 21, 2016 - by Draug

Ruiner is an isometric shooter set in a cyberpunk world of the future, combining grim atmosphere with colourful and detailed graphics (powered by Unreal Engine 4), which bears a slight resemblance to comic book aesthetics. It is the first project from independent team Reikon Games, founded by some of the people who gave us Dying Light and The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. Ruiner is created for PC (Windows, Mac, Linux) and will be released sometime in 2016 (with the assistance of Devolver Digital).

The Settlers: Kingdoms of Anteria is now Champions of Anteria, but Settlers franchise isn't done yet

NewsApril 21, 2016 - by luckie

Under the wave of criticism, Ubisoft decided to transform The Settlers: Kingdoms of Anteria into a less-strategy-more-RPG game. Champions of Anteria, as the project was renamed, is going to feature party-based missions, character progression, and real-time combat with active pause as well as MMO-like boss fights. It will also borrow some elements from The Settlers series, such as kingdom management mechanics pushed in between missions and light-hearted humor. The game is slated for release this summer.

This War of Mine dev announces Beat Cop – an 80s story about a New York cop framed for murder

NewsApril 21, 2016 - by luckie

Beat Cop is a pixel art adventure game from 11 bit studios, the devs of This War of Mine. You play as a cop framed for murder and hunted by the local mafia. Apart from following his daily routine, he needs to turn every stone and find out who set him up. The creators are promising a non-linear story with multiple endings. Beat Cop will be overflowing with the atmosphere and humor of the 1980s as well as inspirations driven from classic American crime television series, such as Kojak, Columbo, Magnum, and Miami Vice.
