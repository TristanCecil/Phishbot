The e-edition is a digital replica of the print edition, so it includes all the same quality stories that you have come to expect from The Plain Dealer in a familiar, user-friendly layout.

You can access the e-edition through a browser on any device that has an Internet connection: tablet, e-reader, smartphone, desktop, laptop. You can browse through the paper, search multiple editions, share, save and much more all at home, at work or even on the move!
