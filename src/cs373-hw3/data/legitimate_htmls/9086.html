Summer registration instructions

Registration for summer courses is now open to all students. Currently enrolled students should log into eServices to register. New students should follow these instructions for registration. For more information, including the summer studies calendar, visit the summer studies website.

Fall registration instructions

Undergraduate students register in accordance with earned hours. To determine your registration time, log into eServices and locate your earned hours on your undergraduate transcript where it says “Transcript Totals (Undergraduate) Earned Hours/Overall.”

Registration begins at 8 a.m. on each date.

Tip

The online Schedule Planner can be a big help when figuring out your schedule. To use it, log into eServices, click the “Student” tab, then “Registration,” then “Schedule Planner.”
