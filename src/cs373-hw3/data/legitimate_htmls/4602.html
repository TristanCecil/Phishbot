Go ahead, get out your checklist of must-haves & deal-breakers

Security and privacy

Security is #1. HipChat's secure conversations are transferred in 256-bit SSL encryption – the same security protocol as online banks.

Secure guest access

Bring clients, vendors, and other third-parties into a HipChat room. They'll only see what you want them to see. You have complete control.

Easy screen sharing

For when you need to show, not tell. Share your desktop with your colleagues on any device to collaborate in real time.

Drag and drop file sharing

Grab and share files from your desktop, Dropbox, email, or anywhere. HipChat shows a preview of the file to everyone in the room.

Unlimited chat rooms

Create rooms for projects, teams, clients, or the lunchtime basketball league. When you're finished with a chat room just archive it.

Fully customizable

Customize how HipChat alerts you when you miss a message. Create a combination of in-app, email, SMS, and mobile push notifications.

Fully searchable

Every room is searchable by keyword, including text, links, files, and more. If it's discussed in HipChat, it's saved forever. We got you.

Persistent

HipChat synchronizes across desktop, mobile, and web apps. That means conversations are never lost when you switch devices.

Time traveling

Just kidding. But having a fully searchable history is sort of like time travel...
