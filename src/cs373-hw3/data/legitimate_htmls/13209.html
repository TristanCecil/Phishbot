primosearch.com



1. What is primosearch.com?

primosearch.com is an advertising network technology tracking domain. Advertisers distribute their ads across a broad network of search partners and content sites. Before traffic is delivered to advertisers, primosearch.com is used as a network referral ID to identify and qualify traffic sent by traffic partners.




2. Why am I seeing primosearch.com on my computer?

Because the advertising network is comprised of thousands of traffic partners, you may have been included within a partner.s advertisement distribution network. If you have received any advertisements that contain the url primosearch.com, it is important to understand that this property and the advertisements you.ve seen have no association with the traffic partner that is displaying ads to you. primosearch.com is not the source of the advertisements.
