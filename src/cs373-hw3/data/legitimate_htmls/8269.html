How Rent the Runway Works

Follow These Simple Steps
1
Shop for Any Occasion
Filter styles by date, size, occasion and more. You have access to more than 350 designers and thousands of pieces.
2
Find the Best Fit
Add a free backup size and a second style for $32.50. If your dress isnt perfect, exchanges are quick and easy.
3
Select a Timeframe
Choose a delivery date and rent for 4 or 8 days. Its best to select a delivery date 1- 2 days before your event.
4
Return for Free
Drop everything off at a UPS store or drop box on your return date. We handle the dry cleaning.
