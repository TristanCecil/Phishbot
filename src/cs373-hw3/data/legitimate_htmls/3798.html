Recent News - Some Merging/Retiring Soon - View More News

Hey everyone! We have a fairly large shakeup coming. Some of our websites that aren't very popular are going to be retired, or merged into other properties. The list of websites that haven't gotten much love in the last 6 months is far too long, and we are reducing that number to make things a little more managable.

  • Meme Abyss is going to merged into Image Abyss.
  • Quote Abyss is going to be retired

Meme Abyss doesn't get very submissions - and that type of content fits fine at Image Abyss, so we are planning on moving Meme Abyss content over to Image Abyss, where hopefully we will actually see more memes being submitted :)

Quote Abyss is being retired. I know a few of you enjoy it - but unfortunately it literally is only a few of you. Every other property we have gets 10x-10000x the traffic of Quote Abyss. The quote data will be saved. If our level of community discussions ever increased significantly, and there's demand, we can bring it back fairly easily.
