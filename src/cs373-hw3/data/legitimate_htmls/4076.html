Knowledge connects the dots

Libraries connect people to the information they need to solve problems, push boundaries and shape the future. OCLC, a global library cooperative, supports thousands of libraries in making information more accessible and more useful to people around the world.

A breakthrough in purpose.

“Our vision is to champion Philippine specialty coffee and support Filipino coffee producers. The spark for that vision came from a library.”

Carmel Laurino, Co-Founder Kalsada Coffee

Inspired by digitized photographs of early 20th century Filipino coffee shops, the founders of Kalsada Coffee became champions of modern-day Filipino coffee growers.
