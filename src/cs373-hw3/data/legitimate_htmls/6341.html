Nonprofit heritage

With TIAA, youre with a financial partner that shares your values. For nearly 100 years, weve worked to make a difference in the lives of the people who power the nonprofit community. Weve helped millions of customers live financially secure lives. And were ready for another century of serving.

Consistent performance

With TIAA, your money works harder. That’s because our fund expenses and fees are generally half of the industry average1. Plus, TIAA has been recognized by Thomson Reuters Lipper as the Best Overall Large Fund Company2 for a record four straight years. So, what does this mean? Your money can go farther and so can you.

Personal service for all
