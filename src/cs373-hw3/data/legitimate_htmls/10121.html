About World Map

This latest world map has been thoroughly updated with all the newly formed countries. Besides depicting countries, international borders, oceans, and other geographical features, this large word map also shows location of small countries through numbers. More than 30 such countries have been indexed for the user's convenience. Clear cartography, use of bold colors, and easily readable fonts make it user-friendly. This high-resolution world map is available in AI, EPS, and layered PDF editable formats. You can add locations, change fonts, colors, and text size on this customizable world map that can be used for printing posters, cartographic work, presentations, classroom teaching, environment projects, etc. Click on any country on this digital world map and get specific map and information about that country.
